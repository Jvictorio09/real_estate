Lesson 13

{{ lesson.subtitle|default:lesson.title }}

Learning sticks when you use it. Apply the Do’s & Don’ts in the first 24–72 hours to cement the habit.

{{ lesson.meta.duration|default:"6–10 mins" }} {{ lesson.meta.level|default:"All Agents" }} Transfer Task

Purpose

Move knowledge from theory into practice within three days of completing this course.

Task Requirements

1) Explain the Rules to Someone Else

Explain 2–3 Do’s and Don’ts to a friend/colleague. Include the reason behind each.

  • Don’t let movers disconnect a fridge water line — leaks can cause $50k damage and won’t be covered by insurance.
  • Do test appliances before the move — prevents false blame if something was already broken.

2) Share a Reflection

  • Was it easy to explain?
  • Did they “get it”?
  • What part had the biggest impact?

3) Identify a Real Client Risk

Think of a recent/upcoming move; note at least one “mover don’t” (e.g., chandelier, plants, valuables) and plan to prevent it using scripts and job aids.

4) Engage with Peers

Respond to at least one other Realtor’s reflection to reinforce boundary-setting across the team.
