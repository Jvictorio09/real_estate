A. Do’s & Don’ts Quick Guide

Download PDF

A. Do’s & Don’ts Quick Guide

A one-page handout that condenses the top 15 rules into a checklist.

Do’s

  • Pack and label electronics yourself.
  • Photograph valuables and test appliances before moving.
  • Confirm mover insurance and claims process.
  • Prepare a “day-of essentials” box.
  • Tip movers fairly.

Don’ts

  • Ask movers to disconnect water, gas, or electrical lines.
  • Expect movers to transport plants, propane, or jewelry.
  • Assume specialty furniture will be handled without prior agreement.
  • Micromanage the process.
  • Store valuables in the moving truck.

B. Mover Boundaries Script

B. Mover Boundaries Script

“Movers move furniture and boxes. Licensed professionals handle water, gas, and electrical work. Valuables and prohibited items stay with the client. This protects everyone—your belongings, the movers, and your home.”
